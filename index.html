How It Works

  • AI Interpretation: Analyzes patterns, shapes, and data trends.
  • Intelligent Simplification: Removes clutter while preserving meaning.
  • Tactile Rendering: Outputs variable-height embossed graphics readable by touch—like a relief.

Our AI Technology

At the heart of Tactile Horizons AI is a patented AI engine that doesn’t just convert images—it understands them. Unlike traditional tactile graphics made from static molds, our system uses deep learning to interpret visual semantics and translate them into intuitive, touch-readable reliefs.

Pattern Recognition

Identifies shapes, data trends, color gradients, and spatial relationships—even in complex infographics or financial charts.

Intelligent Simplification

Filters out visual noise while preserving essential meaning—ensuring tactile outputs are clear, not cluttered.

Precise Calibration

Maps visual intensity to variable-height embossing, so a dark bar in a chart feels taller than a light one—adding a new dimension to touch.

User testing confirms: People with vision loss understand content significantly better with our tactile graphics than with Braille-only text. This isn’t theory—it’s validated impact.

Our AI runs in the cloud and will soon be available as an on-demand web application—allowing educators, publishers, and individuals to upload any image and instantly receive a tactile-ready file for embossing.

Impact

  • Higher STEM Enrollment: Empowers blind students to pursue science and math.
  • Increased Workplace Literacy: Enables professionals to access charts independently.
  • Reduced Public Costs: Lowers government spending on alternate-format materials.
