The Proposition

Tactile Horizons AI transforms visual STEM content—graphs, molecular structures, geometric diagrams—into optimized tactile graphics in 90 seconds, compared to 2-4 weeks for manual production. We are the API-first layer that makes digital learning platforms instantly accessible.

Our Vision: Become the infrastructure layer for accessible STEM content. The API every LMS, publisher, and government portal uses to make visual information accessible.

The Problem

1.3 Billion

People live with vision impairment globally (WHO)

90%

Of STEM content is visual (charts, diagrams, data)

$33 Billion

Annual cost of vision loss in Canada (Deloitte, 2021)

The Gap: Schools and publishers face regulatory deadlines (ADA Title II WCAG 2.1 AA compliance by April 2026) without scalable solutions. Manual production cannot meet demand. AI can.

The Solution

Our three-step automation process ensures high fidelity and speed:

  1. AI Interpretation: Computer vision isolates STEM-relevant features using fine-tuned multimodal models.
  2. Intelligent Simplification: Removes visual clutter while preserving semantic meaning (80% accuracy for standard charts).
  3. Tactile Optimization: Outputs variable-height emboss files calibrated for fingertip discrimination.

Delivery: Cloud API + SaaS dashboard. We integrate with existing Learning Management Systems (Canvas, Blackboard) and embosser hardware (ViewPlus, Index).
