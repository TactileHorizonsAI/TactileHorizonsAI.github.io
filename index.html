Touch the Future

Tactile Horizons AI — Revolutionizing accessibility for the visually impaired

The Problem

Imagine being excluded from charts, images, and essential visual learning. This is the daily reality for over 250 million people with vision loss.

Financial charts, educational diagrams, informative images — all inaccessible, creating a massive social and economic divide.

Our Solution

We are developing AI that bridges the gap between visual data and non-visual interpretation by converting digital images into tactile graphics — touchable reliefs with variable-height embossing.

250M

People with vision loss worldwide

$2B

Global braille & tactile media market

How It Works

  • AI Interpretation: Analyzes patterns, shapes, and data trends in images.
  • Intelligent Simplification: Removes clutter while preserving core meaning.
  • Tactile Rendering: Outputs variable-height embossed graphics readable by touch — like Braille, but for complex visuals.

Impact

  • Higher STEM Enrollment: Empowers blind students in science and math.
  • Increased Workplace Literacy: Professionals can interpret charts and graphs independently.
  • Reduced Public Costs: Lowers government spending on alternate-format materials.

Our Vision

Deploy an AI-powered web application and publish a monthly printed magazine in Braille + tactile graphics — featuring educational, financial, and entertaining content written by and for people with vision disabilities.

Championing 250 million voices — from their perspective.

Business Model

  • Magazine Subscriptions: Yearly institutional sales to ~300,000 schools/libraries (70% gross margin).
  • Licensing: SaaS model — license our AI engine to publishers per page.

Funding Ask: $225K to launch cloud app + embosser hardware, reach break-even in Year 2.

Projected 55% EBITDA within three years. No direct competitors. Premium value.
