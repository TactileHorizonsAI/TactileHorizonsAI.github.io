We’re developing AI that bridges the gap between visual data and non-visual interpretation by converting digital images into tactile graphics with variable-height embossing.

How It Works

  • AI Interpretation: Analyzes patterns, shapes, and data trends.
  • Intelligent Simplification: Removes clutter while preserving meaning.
  • Tactile Rendering: Outputs variable-height embossed graphics readable by touch—like a relief.

Impact

  • Higher STEM Enrollment: Empowers blind students in science and math.
  • Increased Workplace Literacy: Professionals interpret charts independently.
  • Reduced Public Costs: Lowers government spending on alternate formats.

Our Vision

Launch an AI-powered web app and a monthly printed magazine in Braille + tactile graphics—featuring educational, financial, and entertaining content written by and for people with vision disabilities.

Championing 250 million voices—from their perspective.
