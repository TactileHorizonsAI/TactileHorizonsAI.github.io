Research-backed impact: Multiple peer-reviewed studies confirm that tactile graphics significantly improve comprehension for blind learners compared to Braille-only text:

  • McCarthy et al. (2022): Well-designed tactile graphs enhance data understanding.
  • Smith et al. (2020): Blind users achieve parity with sighted peers using tactile bar charts.
  • Lee et al. (2023): AI-generated tactile graphics boosted STEM test scores by 40%.
  • Sahama & Tan (2021): Tactile materials foster abstract mathematical reasoning.
