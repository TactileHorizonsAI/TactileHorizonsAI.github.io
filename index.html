Our Mission

Touch the Future. We are dedicated to transforming how visually impaired individuals access technical, educational, and instructional content. Our patent-pending AI engine converts digital images into intelligent tactile graphics, making complex visual information accessible through touch.

We are changing the story for the 1.2 million Canadians living with vision loss. By removing barriers, we open doors to dignity, independence, and possibility. Talent is universal; opportunity should be too.

The Challenge

The Workforce Gap: While 80% of Canadians without disabilities find employment, only 59% of those with disabilities do. This 21-point gap represents hundreds of thousands of untapped talents. Our technology addresses the $4.3 billion lost to productivity annually by enabling independent performance in technical roles.

The Solution

Tactile Horizons AI operates as a specialized service provider partnering with governments and businesses to convert technical publications into accessible tactile formats.

3-Step Automation Process

  1. AI Interpretation: Computer vision isolates shapes, trends, and color gradients using our patent-pending algorithm.
  2. Intelligent Simplification: Removes visual clutter while preserving core semantic meaning.
  3. Tactile Rendering: Outputs variable-height emboss files calibrated for optimal fingertip discrimination.

Performance: 90-second turnaround vs. 2-4 weeks for manual production. 90% cost reduction.
